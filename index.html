About Us

How It All Began

"Great food starts with great passion"

In 2005, our founder Abel Tewodros transformed his grandmother's recipes into a beloved food culture. What began as a small kitchen in Addis Ababa has grown into a beloved food company across the world.

Our Inspiration

  • Family recipes passed down through generations
  • Locally sourced, organic ingredients
  • Traditional cooking methods
